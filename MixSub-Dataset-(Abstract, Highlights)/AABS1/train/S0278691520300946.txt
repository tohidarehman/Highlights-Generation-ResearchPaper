 Redox biology and toxicology are interrelated fields that have produced valuable evidence regarding the role and clinical significance of reactive species . These issues are analyzed herein by presenting 6 arguments as follows

@highlight Reactive species RS formation is the outcome of numerous chronic diseases.
@highlight Acute intoxications leading to severe illnesses are linked to effects of RS.
@highlight No cause effect relation of RS with chronic diseases has been established yet.
@highlight The real life exposure scenario offers insight in the clinical significance of RS.
