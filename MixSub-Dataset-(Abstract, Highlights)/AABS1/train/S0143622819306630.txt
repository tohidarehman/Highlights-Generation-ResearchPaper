 Improving farmers market accessibility is increasingly being used as a mechanism to promote healthy food consumption . Previous studies about farmers markets largely focused on describing either the demographics of market patrons or the impact of market participation on fruit and vegetable intake . Thus little empirical research has examined how market accessibility impacts nearby communities .
