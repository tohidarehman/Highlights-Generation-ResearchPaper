 A new method using liquid chromatography coupled to mass spectrometry for the detection of fourteen natural and synthetic hormones in muscles was validated in other bovine matrices according to the Decision Commission 2002 657 EC . As result this method demonstrates good linearity R

@highlight Quick LCMS MS method was developed for hormones analysis in bovine matrices.
@highlight The method provided high recoveries and low detection limits.
@highlight Matrix effect was calculated for all analytes in the different matrices.
@highlight Method was successfully applied to detect hormones in eighty eight real samples.
