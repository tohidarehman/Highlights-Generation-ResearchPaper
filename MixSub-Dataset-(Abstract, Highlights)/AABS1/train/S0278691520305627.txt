 Food based dietary guidelines are developed to promote appropriate nutrients intake . However FBDGs may trigger higher exposure to some food chemical contaminants while recommending the consumption of specific food groups that are more contaminated than others . In some cases the balance between benefits and risks is difficult to achieve .
