 Rapid population aging is underway across the entirety of Canada . With the proportion of older adults increasing and the majority of the older generation planning to age in place understanding how older adult vulnerability differs spatially is paramount to developing targeted policy interventions and creating a more age friendly society . Our study examines the locational differences of older adult vulnerability through statistical and spatial analysis . A nationwide neighbourhood analysis of Canada
