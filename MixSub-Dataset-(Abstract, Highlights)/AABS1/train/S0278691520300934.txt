 Alternative therapies with new drugs are needed because the clinical efficacy of conventional chemotherapy is often reduced due to collateral effects . Many natural products of plant origin including essential oils have proved to be effective in prevention and therapy of several diseases such as bacterial infections chronic diseases and cancer .
