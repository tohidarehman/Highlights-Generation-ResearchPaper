 Evapotranspiration plays an important role in efficient irrigation and water resources management . ET can be accurately estimated via the FAOPenmanMonteith method ET

@highlight The simplicity and predictability of ET models are important in practical application.
@highlight The temperature based ET methods showed better performance than the radiation based ET methods.
@highlight The best method for estimating ET differed among growing seasons in 54 of areas.
@highlight The best estimates were obtained using the radiation based methods for warm growing season.
@highlight The temperature based method was the best for estimating ET during cool growing seasons.
