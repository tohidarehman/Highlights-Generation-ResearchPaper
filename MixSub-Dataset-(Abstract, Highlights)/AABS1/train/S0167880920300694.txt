 The occurrence and dissemination of antibiotic resistance genes constitute a major concern worldwide and pose crucial threats to public health . The current study focused on this issue in the agricultural soils of Wuliangsuhai watershed in China . A total of 33 ARGs belonging to 8 major types were identified in the soil samples
