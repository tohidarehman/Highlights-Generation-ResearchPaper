 The concept of place links people to their environment and is foundational to disciplines such as geography environmental psychology and urban studies . With growth in geographic information systems in the 1990s research began to operationalize place concepts using GIS to better inform land use decisions . After two decades participatory mapping has emerged as an important method to identify place values .
