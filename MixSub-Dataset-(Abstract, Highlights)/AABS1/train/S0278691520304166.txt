 Malachite Green has a worldwide application in aquaculture as a therapeutic agent however its use in food producing animals is illegal due to potential carcinogenicity and persistence of residues . This pilot study the first conducted in Armenia aimed to determine the concentration of MG residues in flesh of fish grown in artificial ponds of Armenia and conduct dietary exposure assessment to characterize possible health risks to consumers .
