 Novel benzothiazole based compounds were designed and synthesized as potential antimicrobial agents with dual DNA gyrase topoisomerase IV inhibitory activity . The structures of the newly synthesized compounds were established on the basis of spectral and elemental analyses . Most of the studied compounds possessed significant antimicrobial activity against tested bacteria and fungi . Compounds
