 Alkaline phosphatases are membrane bound metalloenzymes distributed all over the body . Recent studies have revealed that by targeting ALPs can lead towards the treatment of many deadliest diseases including cardiac cancerous and brain diseases . Thioureas and their derivatives are of considerable significance and are privileged scaffolds in medicinal chemistry . They show a wide range of pharmacological activities such as antibacterial antiparasitic anti inflammatory and antioxidants
