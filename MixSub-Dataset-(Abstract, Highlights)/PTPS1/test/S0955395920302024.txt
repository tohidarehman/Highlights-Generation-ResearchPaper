 Although people who use drugs are a high risk group for tuberculosis there is practically no data on TB prevalence in Ivory Coast . The aim of the study was to estimate pulmonary TB prevalence and assess the cascade of care with confirmed pulmonary TB among PWUD in Abidjan .

@highlight There is a high prevalence of tuberculosis among the PWUD population in Abidjan.
@highlight A systematic TB testing and follow up program is feasible using a community based approach with a high acceptability among PWUD.
@highlight It is crucial to develop prevention programs tailored to these population ant to fight against discrimination and stigmatization.
