 This study examined the effects of the iPlayClean anti doping intervention on attitudes towards doping and susceptibility and whether delivery mode affected the results .

@highlight Favourable attitudes towards doping can be reduced and maintained for at least 8 weeks.
@highlight The mode of delivery does not impact the effects of the intervention on doping attitudes.
@highlight Perceptions of doping susceptibility can be reduced immediately after education.
@highlight The mode of delivery impacts the sustainability of effects of the intervention on doping susceptibility.
@highlight Face to face presentation mode of delivery had more sustainable affects than the online or presentation with online access 8 weeks later.
