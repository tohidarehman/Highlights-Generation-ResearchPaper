 The 5th cycle of the German Environmental Survey investigated the internal human exposure of children and adolescents aged 317 years in Germany to per and polyfluoroalkyl substances . The fieldwork of the population representative GerES V was performed from 2014 to 2017 .

@highlight This study provides representative blood plasma levels on PFAS for the German population aged 317 years.
@highlight Despite a decline of emissions the young generation is substantially exposed to these ubiquitous contaminants.
@highlight Observed differences in exposures require follow up investigations.
