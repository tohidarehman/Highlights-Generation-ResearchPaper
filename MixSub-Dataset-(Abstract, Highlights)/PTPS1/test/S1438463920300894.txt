 2 Mercaptobenzothiazole is widely used as a vulcanisation accelerator and is contained in many products made from natural rubber e.g . car tires . Additionally it is used as a fungicide in paint or fibre . Systemically human exposure to 2 MBT can occur via dermal and oral uptake or inhalation . Locally 2 MBT can cause skin sensitisation . The International Agency for Research on Cancer classified 2 MBT as probably carcinogenic to humans .
