 The authentication and traceability of craft beers is an important issue for both beer consumers and producers . Reliable analytical methods able to identify and discriminate products are needed to protect the craft brew market against fraud and counterfeit .

@highlight A method for rapid beer metabolites determination from NMR spectra is described.
@highlight H NMR combined with chemometric methods discriminates craft and industrial beers.
@highlight Both untargeted and targeted analyses were effective in affording the separation.
@highlight The metabolites responsible for the discrimination were identified.
