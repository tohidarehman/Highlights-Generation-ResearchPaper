 The utilization of hydrogels derived from biopolymers as solid electrolyte of electrochemical supercapacitors is a topic of increasing interest because of their promising applications in biomedicine

@highlight Building blocks have been photo crosslinked to prepare a polyesteramide hydrogel.
@highlight The morphology of the biodegradable and biocompatible hydrogel favors ion transport.
@highlight Its ionic conductivity is higher than that of conventional gel polymer electrolytes.
@highlight It exhibits excellent performance as electrolyte for electrochemical supercapacitors.
@highlight The hydrogel has great potential for self powered biomedical implants.
