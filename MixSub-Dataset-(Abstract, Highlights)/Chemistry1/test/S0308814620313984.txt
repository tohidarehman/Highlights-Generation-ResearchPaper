 Some halophyte plants are currently used in gourmet cuisine due to their unique organoleptic properties . Moreover they exhibit excellent nutritional and functional properties being rich in polyphenolics and vitamins . These compounds are associated to strong antioxidant activity and enhanced health benefits . This work compared the nutritional properties and antioxidant potential of three species
