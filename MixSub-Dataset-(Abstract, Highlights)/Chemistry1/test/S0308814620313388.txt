 The phenolic compound hydroxytyrosol and its derivatives are responsible for some of the health benefits of the intake of virgin olive oil having shown antiangiogenic properties . In this study we explored the antiangiogenic potential of six synthetic hydroxytyrosyl alkyl ethers . Our results showed that all compounds affected endothelial cell viability
