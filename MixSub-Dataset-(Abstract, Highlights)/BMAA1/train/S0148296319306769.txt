 Scholars and practitioners often consider the perfect gift to understand the qualities of the most cherished gifts or to encourage givers to choose such gifts . Whilst useful this represents a utopian view of gift giving . This study explores the concept of best gift ever through 35 in depth interviews in order to understand the properties of significant gifts as defined by givers and receivers .
