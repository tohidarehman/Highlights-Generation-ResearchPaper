 Knowledge transfer and exchange has become an integral part of organizational practice . Evaluation of KTE as well as knowledge products generated through this process is important for understanding the effectiveness of KTE strategies . This scoping review aimed to identify tools and frameworks used to evaluate knowledge uptake and utilization .

@highlight Evaluation of knowledge uptake and utilization is a crucial process for understanding the use and effectiveness specific knowledge products
@highlight Only 23 of the tools uncovered could be used to evaluate knowledge uptake and utilization of knowledge products processes across disciplines.
@highlight Of these 23 tools only two were informed by theory the Knowledge Uptake and Utilization Tool and the Level of Knowledge Use Survey.
