 With the deterioration of the human living environment the consumption of eco friendly products has received increasing attention specifically with regard to morality . Given the positive metaphorical associations between morality and up position up displayed eco friendly products can enhance consumers positive responses to such products . More importantly we find that the up position enhancement effect depends on consumers psychological distance from environmental issues .
