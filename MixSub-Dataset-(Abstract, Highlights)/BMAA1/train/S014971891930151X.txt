 Empirical evidence on community driven child health promotion programs in disadvantaged migrant populations is limited despite various promotional strategies . Therefore we implemented a developmental process to shape child health interventions using theory guided community based participatory action research in a migrant community in Bishkek Kyrgyzstan between 2015 and 2019 .

@highlight Migrant children are disadvantaged in availability and accessibility of health care services.
@highlight An ecological model of an enabling environment of nurturing care was employed to guide CBPAR process.
