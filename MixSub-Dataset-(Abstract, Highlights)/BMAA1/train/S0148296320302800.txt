 We report a study designed to determine the most efficient means of pursuing sonic seasoning in international marketing . For the first time music chosen to trigger specific emotional responses was directly and cross culturally compared with music chosen as crossmodally congruent with specific taste flavors .

@highlight This study assessed the most efficient way to pursue sonic seasoning in multisensory marketing.
@highlight Music that trigger emotions was compared to music crossmodally congruent with flavor attributes.
@highlight Effects of emotional music were more prominent than those of the crossmodally corresponding music.
@highlight Sonic seasoning in marketing should therefore mainly aim at classifying music based on emotions.
