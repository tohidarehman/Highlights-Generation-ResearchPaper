 Loyalty is key for sharing economy platforms as their success depends on gaining and retaining a critical mass of users . Using a sequential design of quantitative and mixed methods a sample of 208 users of SE platforms was analysed . The results indicate that loyalty is achieved at the intersection of website organisation platform responsiveness and reliability and customer interaction with the peer service provider . Three strategies are proposed to achieve loyalty each comprising two out of the three mentioned antecedents . Sensitivity analysis of the loyalty antecedents suggests specific customer segments .
