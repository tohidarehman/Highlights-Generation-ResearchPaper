 Due to several ecosystem services provided to the community the multifunctional management of forests has acquired an important role over the years .

@highlight This study analyze a set of socio economic indicators related to different forest management options.
@highlight Using PCA the correlations between the indicators were analysed.
@highlight Results show differences among treatments and the possible correlations between the indicators.
@highlight The indicators used has proven to be effective when carrying out an evaluation of the existing types of forest management.
