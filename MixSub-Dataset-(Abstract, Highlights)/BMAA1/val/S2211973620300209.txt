 Academic discourse supporting the inclusion of spirituality in higher education has grown across many disciplines . Scholars are arguing for the integration of spirituality into the contemporary intellectual landscape . However the significance of spirituality to tourism higher education has not been adequately understood and little research has been undertaken .
