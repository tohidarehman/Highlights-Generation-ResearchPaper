 Tourism resilience studies often focus on a single shock event . In reality the same destination may face different kinds of shocks . It is important to compare the relative effect and resilience to different shocks .

@highlight We study the effect of shocks on domestic and foreign tourism in 22 Indian states.
@highlight Natural disasters have a significant negative effect on foreign tourists.
@highlight Severity of political conflict significantly negatively affects domestic tourists.
@highlight More states suffered breaks in foreign compared to domestic tourist arrival trends.
@highlight Time to recover from shocks was typically larger for foreign tourist arrivals.
