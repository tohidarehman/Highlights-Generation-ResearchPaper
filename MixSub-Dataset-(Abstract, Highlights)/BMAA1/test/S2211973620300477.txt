 The aim of this article is to analyse the extent to which zenith images can be used to study visitor behaviour in crowded spaces . The paper presents two studies of zenith image capture in two micro spaces in the north east of Spain the first used drone images to conduct a quantitative study on patterns of visitor use at Empries archaeological site and the second was a qualitative study on conflicts of use in Cathedral Square Girona using time lapse photography from a fixed camera .
