 This study utilized a multilevel sample of 543 employees and 106 managers and structural equation modelling to test the proposed hypotheses . The results showed the mediating role of the group level work environment in the relationships between transformational leadership and individual level social capital and human capital . Moreover social capital and knowledge transfer mediate the relationship between human capital and creative behaviour . Theoretical and managerial implications are discussed .
