 Acute pancreatitis has been recognized as an uncommon yet potentially lethal complication after hematopoietic stem cell transplant . This retrospective nested case control study reviewed data from 5284 consecutive patients who underwent allogeneic HSCT between 2009 and 2018 at a single center identifying 40 patients with AP after allo HSCT .
