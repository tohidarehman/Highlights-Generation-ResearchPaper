 A high performance p GaN NiO nanostructures n GaN ultraviolet sandwich structure photodetector was fabricated that was composed of NiO nanostructures grown on n GaN and a p GaN film layer . The device based on the GaN p GaN NiO nanostructures n GaN sandwich structure showed a high responsivity and fast response . This study provides a method to fabricate high response UV photodetectors for GaN based materials by combining them with metal oxide nanostructures .
