 This paper aims to examine some challenges related to economic aspects of Spanish maritime goods transport firms . Concretely it is focused on assessing the factors that determine Spanish shipping firms survival .

@highlight Challenges for the sustainable management of maritime goods transport firms are examined.
@highlight The empirical analysis is based on non parametric and semi parametric techniques.
@highlight Liquid bulk Ro Ro and container firms have better survival prospects.
@highlight Positive effect of financial strength and negotiation power on the survival.
@highlight Increasing recognition of the role of maritime transport.
