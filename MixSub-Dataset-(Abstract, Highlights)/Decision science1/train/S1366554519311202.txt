 This study focuses on the transformation of logistics centers in Industry 4.0 . Aim is to reveal the important criteria for logistics centers in Industry 4.0 by considering link to traditional logistics centers practices and proposing a framework for new logistics centers . Initially literature review to reveal criteria for logistics centers in Industry 4.0 is conducted . Secondly fuzzy multi criteria decision making methodology is used to present the importance order and the causal relationship between criteria to make recommendations for future implications . The results may be useful for logistics centers professionals in transition process and new research topics may be derived for academics .
