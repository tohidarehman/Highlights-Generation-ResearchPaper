 Motivated by United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and the importance of sustainability this study examines how the textile and apparel supply chains can comply with the SDGs . By examining the literature as well as industrial practices we show that the current sustainable operations in TA industry are far away from realizing the goals of economic growth going hand in hand with the social and environmental sustainability .
