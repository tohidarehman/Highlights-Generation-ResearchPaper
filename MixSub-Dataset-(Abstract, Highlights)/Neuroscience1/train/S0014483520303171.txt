 Dry eye syndrome is a common disease associated to eyes inflammation irritation and tear film instability . The enzymatic complex of xanthine oxidoreductase is involved in the generation of reactive oxygen species and uric acid that in the end can cause reperfusion injuries irritation and pathological conditions . Furthermore in the eye it has been proposed that oxygen free radicals might play a significant role in retinal ischemic damage .
