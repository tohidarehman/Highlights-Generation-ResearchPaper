 People who stutter suffer from stereotypes portraying them as timid and anxious which may affect their relationships and careers . One of the mechanisms for this stereotyping is the anchoring and adjustment heuristic whereby individuals make judgements about PWS by using previous experiences for the initial judgement and then adjust accordingly .
