 Expectations of satiation and satiety have been increasingly investigated because of the interest in how they along with liking can modulate portion size selection . Consumer characteristics can also be important when consumers select their portion size . However the contribution and interaction of consumers and product aspects to portion size selection has not been unveiled . This study aims to better understanding these complex relations by simultaneously assessing the relative influence of consumer characteristics and product related properties on portion size selection utilizing PLS Path Modelling approach .
