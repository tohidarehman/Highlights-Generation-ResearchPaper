 Social anxiety is quite common and associated with multiple comorbidities . Here we examined the effects of working memory training on various indices potentially related to SA . Pre selected university students with elevated self reported SA symptoms were assigned to a WM training

@highlight Trained students showed changes in event related potentials ERPs to facial stimuli.
