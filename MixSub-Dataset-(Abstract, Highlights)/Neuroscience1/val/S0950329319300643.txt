 Consumers around the globe are showing an increasing demand for food products that are perceived as being natural . The present study aimed to assess what properties of wine have an influence on the perceived naturalness of wine with a focus on winemaking techniques . An online survey was conducted in the German speaking part of Switzerland

@highlight Wine naturalness was more important to Swiss compared to Australian respondents.
@highlight An Old World origin aging in barriques or oak corks were perceived as the most natural properties of wine.
@highlight The addition of sugar sulfites or the use of gelatin were perceived as the most unnatural wine properties.
@highlight Additives were perceived as significantly less natural than processing aids and technologies.
