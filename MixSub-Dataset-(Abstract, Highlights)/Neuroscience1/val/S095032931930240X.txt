 As an extension of the current strategies employed to evaluate new products attributes this research presents a more realistic approach and obtains more accurate results through the combination of real choice experiments and sensory analysis . Two RCEs are carried out to mimic a real shopping scenario before and after experiencing the taste of extra virgin olive oils with a new attribute . Regular EVOO consumers evaluated the products under three different stages
