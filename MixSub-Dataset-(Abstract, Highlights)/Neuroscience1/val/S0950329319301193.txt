 According to the incentive salience hypothesis framework wanting and liking can be dissociated suggesting that two products having similar liking levels could trigger different wanting behaviors in consumers . Defined as a motivational state that promotes approach toward and consumption of rewarding stimuli wanting can be measured through the Pavlovian instrumental transfer procedure . Having emerged from animal models the PIT procedure aims to measure the effort exerted by an organism to obtain a particular reward .
