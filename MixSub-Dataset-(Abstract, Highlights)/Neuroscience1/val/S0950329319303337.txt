 Consumer beliefs play an important role in explaining consumer behavior . This exploratory study aims at building an inventory of consumer beliefs about organic food . To reach this objective we conducted a content analysis of online comments about organic food posted on news websites and forums in German speaking countries and the United States . Such user generated content has emerged as an abundant source of insight for consumer research although very little has been exploited in organic food consumption research .
