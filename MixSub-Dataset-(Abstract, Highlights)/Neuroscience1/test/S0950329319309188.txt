 In human research images of food are often used as cues in place of real foods . To elicit anticipatory responses in targeted populations cultural differences and population norms with regard to food preferences need to be considered . This pilot study aimed to construct two image paradigms for experimental use within the Australian population . A dataset of 200 images from the licenced database
