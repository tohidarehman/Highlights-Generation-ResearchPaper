 This study aimed to further the development and application of the Global profile approach by extending it to another product category alcoholic cocktails investigating the relationship between sensory emotions context appropriateness in consumers differing for liking patterns and integrating the approach beyond product differentiation to consumer differentiation thus improving and gaining a further insight into differences among consumers . This was done taking into account consumer differences in oral responsiveness personality traits familiarity explicit responses to products and implicit attitudes towards brand .
