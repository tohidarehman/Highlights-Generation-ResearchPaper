 This paper investigates the relationship between the choice drivers for wine among Chinese consumers with their personal values using two sets of Best Worst Scaling data .

@highlight The study proposes an innovative way to link choice drivers and personal values.
@highlight Self Enhancement types choose wine based on objective characteristics of the product.
@highlight Self Transcendence Conservativism types rely on experience or suggestions of others.
@highlight Self Transcendence Openness to Change types choose wines suggested by wine authorities.
