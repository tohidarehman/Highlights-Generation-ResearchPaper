 This study conducted a survey at 15 building construction and demolition sites in Hanoi Vietnam in order to identify waste generation rates composition and current handling practices of construction and demolition waste . Waste quantification based on CDW layout image analysis to identify CDW components and face to face interviews with construction and demolition contractors to reveal CDW flows were performed .
