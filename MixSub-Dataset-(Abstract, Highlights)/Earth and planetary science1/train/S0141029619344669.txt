 The fatigue behavior of an adhesively bonded glass fiber reinforced polymer steel double lap joint under cyclic tensile loading with load ratio

@highlight Fatigue behavior of the adhesive GFRP steel DLJ was experimentally investigated.
@highlight Stiffness degradation models applicable to the adhesive GFRP steel DLJ were presented.
@highlight Crack propagation law for the adhesive GFRP steel DLJ was provided.
@highlight The difference between fatigue behavior of the adhesive GFRP steel and GFRP GFRP DLJ was identified.
