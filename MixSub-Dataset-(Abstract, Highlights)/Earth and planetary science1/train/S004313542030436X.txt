 Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses causing a spectrum of disease ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome . The recent outbreak of coronavirus disease 2019 has become a public health emergency worldwide . SARS CoV 2 the virus responsible for COVID 19 is spread by human to human transmission via droplets or direct contact . However since SARS CoV 2 has been found in the fecal samples and anal swabs of some patients the possibility of fecal oral transmission need to be investigated and clarified .
