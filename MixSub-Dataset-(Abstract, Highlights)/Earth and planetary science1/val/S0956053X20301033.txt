 A proper attitude towards clothing close to the end of its life cycle and optimal post consumer textile disposal behaviour have a potential to cause less environmental damage in both global and local perspectives . Only limited data on consumer behaviour toward textile products textile waste and its passage to municipal waste streams are available in the Czech Republic . This paper reports on a survey conducted among 1046 respondents attempting to identify differences in consumer behaviour towards textile products using advanced statistical methods .
