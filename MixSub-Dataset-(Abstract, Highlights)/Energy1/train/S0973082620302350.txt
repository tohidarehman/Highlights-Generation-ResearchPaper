 Liquefied petroleum gas is considered to be a clean burning domestic cooking fuel across the world . The Government of India is promoting use of LPG as cooking fuel among Indian population . Refilling of LPG is not affordable to poor families . It is difficult for the Government to reduce price of LPG cylinders . In such a situation to make LPG affordable it is necessary to investigate methods for efficient utilization of LPG .

@highlight Three common cooking processes are selected for experimentation.
@highlight Round bottom pot is found to be consuming less LPG than flat bottom pot.
@highlight For efficient cooking process optimum pot size is important.
@highlight Each gram of LPG saved by each family will save huge amount of national wealth.
