 Sandy beaches are not roads but they have been used as such worldwide threatening endemic fauna such as ghost crabs . The objective of the present study was to identify the spatial factors influencing the incidence of ghost crabs

@highlight We applied the road ecology approach to investigate vehicle ghost crab collisions.
@highlight Ghost crabs killed by vehicles were found on the beach and on the dirt road.
@highlight Ghost crabs deaths were related with higher vehicle traffic and lower urbanization.
@highlight Surveillance of beach driving is essential for coastal biodiversity conservation.
