 The long held view that birdsong is exclusively a male trait has been challenged recently by a number of studies and reviews highlighting the prevalence of female song . Despite that there remains a lack of knowledge on the function of female song with most evidence thus far focusing on females performing duets with males in courtship displays typically for joint territory defence or mate guarding purposes .
