 Mobile phone use while driving presents significant risks potentially leading to injury or death through distracted driving . Using a case study of Vietnam this research aimed to understand the effect of problematic mobile phone use attitudes and beliefs and perceived risk on the frequency of mobile phone use among motorcyclists and car drivers .
