 This study firstly aimed to describe bicyclists return to cycling after a hospitalisation crash . Secondly it aimed to determine factors associated with reduced cycling post crash . A study of 83 cyclists hospitalised due to an on road crash was undertaken in Perth Western Australia . Participants completed a questionnaire shortly following the crash and were followed up approximately 12 months after the crash . Injury information was obtained from the WA State Trauma Registry .
