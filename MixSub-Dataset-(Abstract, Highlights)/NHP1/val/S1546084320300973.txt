 The prevalence of burnout among US registered nurses ranges from 35 to 45 . In one study nurses had twice the rate of depression compared with other health care professionals . Owing to the Covid 19 pandemic burnout is a major threat to the stability of the workforce on the front lines . Consultation liaison psychiatry can provide assistance through liaison meetings stress management programs and curbside consults to help reduce the risk of burnout . Narrative medicine programs mindfulness based stress reduction and meditation apps are additional means to alleviate stress . Given the current challenges facing C L psychiatry and the mental health field in general there is an urgent need to overcome stigma and financial barriers to make treatment readily accessible .
