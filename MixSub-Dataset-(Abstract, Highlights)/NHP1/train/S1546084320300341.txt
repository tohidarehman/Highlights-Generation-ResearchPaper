 Communicating bad news to a patient or family is as much an art and test of will power as anything . The following article outlines considerations and points of emphasis when performing this task . Also is a list of resources and a case example of how to properly handle an unexpected adverse outcome .

@highlight Good communication with the patient and the family is an essential part of health care.
@highlight Adverse outcome of a procedure is not achieving the desired result after performing the procedure.
@highlight If adverse outcome is to be delivered advanced preparation is essential in transmitting accurate information and in properly setting the conversation up.
