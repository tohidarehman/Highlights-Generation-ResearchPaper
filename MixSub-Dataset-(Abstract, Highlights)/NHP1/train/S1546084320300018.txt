 Cerebrospinal fluid leaks due to spinal procedures are commonly reported and can result in a condition known as intracranial hypotension . In rare cases this can lead to a life threatening situation if not properly diagnosed and treated . This case study discusses a rapidly decompensating patient that was found to have a CSF leak after an epidural placement for childbirth and made a full recovery after treatment with an epidural blood patch .
