 UPMC Children s Hospital of Pittsburgh has made many changes in practice and processes to impact patient satisfaction in our radiology department . The primary aim of this article is to show changes made related to the preparation and care of outpatients to this department focusing on sedation phone calls staff impact and effective patient care . A task force was developed to review and devise scheduling success opportunities . Results included an increase in staff engagement and many changes have been made and upheld to provide an opportunity to increase satisfaction in the outpatient setting of radiology department .
