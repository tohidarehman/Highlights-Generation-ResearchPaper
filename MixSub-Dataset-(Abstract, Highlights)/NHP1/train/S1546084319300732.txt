 Sepsis is a life threatening condition requiring prompt recognition and aggressive multidisciplinary treatment . This treatment consists of but is not limited to cardiovascular support via fluids and vasoactive medications plus systemic broad spectrum antibiotics . This article reviews the guidelines as well as supporting primary literature surrounding fluids vasoactive medications and antibiotic recommendations for pediatric sepsis .
