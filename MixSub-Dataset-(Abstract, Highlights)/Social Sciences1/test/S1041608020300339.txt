 A person centered approach was used to identify the profiles of symptoms of psychological ill being among Finnish upper secondary education students

@highlight A person centered approach was used to examine symptoms of psychological ill being.
@highlight One asymptomatic and three symptomatic profiles were identified among students.
@highlight Approximately 20 of students experienced symptoms of psychological ill being.
@highlight Gender and educational track differences were found in students symptoms.
@highlight Symptomatic students reported the highest levels of school dropout intentions.
