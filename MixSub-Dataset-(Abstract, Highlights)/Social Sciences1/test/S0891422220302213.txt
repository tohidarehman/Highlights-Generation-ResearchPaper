 Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder has been associated with hyperfocus a transient experience of enhanced attentional focus and diminished awareness of time and the environment . This study aims to investigate the association between the frequency duration and pervasiveness of hyperfocus across different situations in adults with and without ADHD .
