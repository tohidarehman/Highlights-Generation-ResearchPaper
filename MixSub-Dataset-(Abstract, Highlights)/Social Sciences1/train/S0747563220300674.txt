 The relationship between self selected fitness application software use and physical activity behavior is not well studied nor is the role that exercise identity may play in predicting fitness app use . This study examined these relationships by evaluating the ability of exercise identity to mediate a hypothesized positive association between fitness app use and physical activity behavior .
