 Research has been focusing increasingly on measuring students prior economic knowledge in higher education . However in German speaking countries valid instruments are rare . A diagnostic study investigated the validity and fairness of an internationally established economic knowledge test with a representative sample of 7664 students from 46 universities across Germany .
