 The tendency to disclose personal information on Facebook has been examined in relation to the broad Big Five personality factors but the potential roles of more specific traits such as alexithymia and impulsivity are not known . The present study assessed the ability of these two traits along with indices of disordered social media use alcohol use negative mood and demographic factors to predict Facebook self disclosure in a hierarchical regression model .
