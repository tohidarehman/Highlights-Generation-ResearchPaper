 Research has suggested that computer mediated communication can be effective in facilitating positive psychosocial outcomes when such interactions increase one s perceived social support . These findings bear valuable implications for sexual minority men who can experience a lack of social support due to their stigmatized identities and thus may turn to social networking sites for identity related support .
