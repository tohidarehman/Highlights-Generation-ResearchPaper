 As online news today is an important source of political information and available in vast quantities understanding its use and its impact on citizens political knowledge is vital . The aim of the present study is to identify different usage patterns of online news and their relation to individually perceived and actually measurable political knowledge . To do so we conducted an online survey of German online news users
