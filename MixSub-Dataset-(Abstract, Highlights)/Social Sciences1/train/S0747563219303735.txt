 The popular business media argues that the fear of missing out on work related opportunities harms employees health and performance . Yet these claims rely on the study of FoMO in college students in non work contexts . Therefore we explored workplace FoMO among employees across three studies . We first developed a measure and provided validation evidence for workplace FoMO among diverse employees
