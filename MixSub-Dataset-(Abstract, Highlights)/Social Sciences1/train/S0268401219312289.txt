 This paper systematically examines how individual personality can influence on cultural acculturation which can impact on the higher level of perceived value in LGBTs and how the higher level of perceived value towards social media can result in being more satisfied consequently having a better working life at workplace and performance proficiency . The generated framework was validated through a survey from multinational corporations in multi industries such as higher education global fashion global retailing tourism hospitality food and electronics and information technology . Key results implications for managers and researchers are highlighted .
