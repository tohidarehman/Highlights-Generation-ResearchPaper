 Given the increased use of performance assessments in higher education to evaluate achievement of learning outcomes it is important to address the barriers related to ensuring quality for this type of assessment . This article presents a design based research study that resulted in the development of a Validity Inquiry Process . The studys aim was to support faculty in examining the validity and reliability of the interpretation and use of results from locally developed PAs . DBR was determined to be an appropriate method because it is used to study interventions such as an instructional innovation type of assessment technology integration or administrative activity
