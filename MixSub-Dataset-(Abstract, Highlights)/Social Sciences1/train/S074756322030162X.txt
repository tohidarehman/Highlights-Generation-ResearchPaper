 This study uses social presence theory and media multiplexity theory to explore Snapchat use and closeness in interpersonal relationships between family members in friendships and in romantic relationships among college students . Snapchat as a multimodal communication technology is integral to understanding the role of multimodal technologies ability to enhance relational closeness and extends prior research by shifting the focus from monomodal to multimodal communication .
