 The purpose of this study was to identify three categories of esports game genres and to examine the impact those genres have on the relationship that exists between antecedents and consumers esports gameplay intention . The hypothesized model was based on the esports consumption model which is grounded in the Unified Theory of Acceptance and Use of Technology 2 .
