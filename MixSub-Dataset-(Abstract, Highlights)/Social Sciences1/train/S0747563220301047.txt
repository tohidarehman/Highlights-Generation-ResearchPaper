 Smartphones provide many advantages for people s daily life which makes the phone become a valuable attachment object . Attachment to a smartphone may have negative psychological consequences such as anxiety about being separated from the smartphone . Considerable research has been done on smartphone separation anxiety . However they are rarely concerned about the impact of smartphone activity on it . The purpose of this study was to examine the effects of mobile activity on smartphone separation anxiety by emphasizing the uniqueness of the phone itself and the importance of the activity . The present study examined the anxiety levels of 1733 Chinese young adults mean age 19.13
