 Exergames have been adopted as a new approach to promote healthy and active aging in recent years . This study aimed to understand the effects of exergames on emotional well being of older adults and further explore factors influencing the two types of affects after playing exergames . The study used a 2 3 mixed factorial design with 319 Singaporean older adults with an average age of 73 years old over six weeks .
