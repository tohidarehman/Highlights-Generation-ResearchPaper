 Mobile social networking sites are frequently theorized to lead to perceived information overload which may affect the well being of individuals in negative ways . However the available body of research is mainly based on cross sectional data . Based on the limited capacity model of motivated mediated message processing we tested the over time relationships between mobile SNS use information overload depressive symptoms and well being in a two wave panel study .
