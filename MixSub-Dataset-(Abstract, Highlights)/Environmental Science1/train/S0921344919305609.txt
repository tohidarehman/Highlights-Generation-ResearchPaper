 China has recently adopted ecological civilization as a new national strategy for sustainable development . Understanding how the new strategy differs from previous national sustainability strategies is critical to evaluate how Chinas eco civilization can contribute to global sustainability . Here we reviewed government administrative system changes under the eco civilization strategy in China . Policy suggestions are provided to further reform the government administrative system for better implementation of eco civilization .
