 Malt and beer related by products namely caramalt aromatic malt roasted malt malt sprouts dark beer spent grains and wheat beer spent grains were extracted with water under moderate conditions to exploit the potential as sources of bioactive compounds . After characterizing the extracts in terms of carbohydrate amino acid and phenolic contents and composition their antioxidant potential was assessed

@highlight Aqueous extracts of malt and beer related by products contain phenolic compounds.
@highlight The phenolic content is positively correlated with antioxidant activity.
@highlight Malt based extracts can inhibit tyrosinase
@highlight Enhanced scavenging of reactive oxygen species is shown in keratinocyte cell lines.
@highlight Data suggests a skin whitening potential of malt based extracts.
