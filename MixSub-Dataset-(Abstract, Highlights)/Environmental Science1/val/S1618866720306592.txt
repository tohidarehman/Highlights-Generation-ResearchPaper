 Green Stormwater Infrastructure is being implemented in cities around the globe . Although we know that GSI improves urban ecosystems in a variety of ways we know little about the extent to which the characteristics of GSI impact human perception and preference . This gap in knowledge necessitates a greater understanding of the relationship between GSI perceptions and preference . Without this knowledge designers and planners risk creating landscapes that people dislike and from which they reap few health benefits .
