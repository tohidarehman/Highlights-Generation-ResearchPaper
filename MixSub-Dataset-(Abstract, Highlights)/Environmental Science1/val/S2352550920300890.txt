 Online shopping makes consumption increasingly easy . It may lead to more sufficiency oriented goods and services being bought but also comes with the risk of overconsumption due to rebound and induction effects . This study examines whether perceived behavioral efficiency gains of online shopping are associated with higher consumption levels of new as well as sufficiency oriented goods or services .
