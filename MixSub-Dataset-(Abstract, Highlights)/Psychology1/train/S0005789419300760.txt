 Affective styles appear to be relevant to the development of psychopathology especially anxiety disorders . The aim of the current study was to investigate changes in affective styles in patients with panic disorder and specific phobia as a result of undergoing cognitive behavioral therapy and to identify a possible link between certain affective styles and remission .
