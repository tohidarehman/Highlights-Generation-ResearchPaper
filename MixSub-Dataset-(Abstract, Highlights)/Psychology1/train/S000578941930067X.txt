 Anxiety and insomnia disorders are two of the most common and costly mental health conditions . They are frequently comorbid but current treatments do not target both . To streamline treatment we developed a computerized intervention targeting a transdiagnostic factor safety aids . We conducted a randomized controlled trial to determine the acceptability and efficacy of this brief one session intervention . Young adult undergraduates
