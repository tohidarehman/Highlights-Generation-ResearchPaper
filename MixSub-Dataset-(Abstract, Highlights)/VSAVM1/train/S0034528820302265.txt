 The vaccine against gonadotropin releasing factor Improvac has recently emerged as an option for rearing market gilts up to heavier harvest weights . Improvac s temporary suppression of the ovarian function and gonadal hormones results in greater feed intake and better welfare from avoidance of sexual behavioral problems .
