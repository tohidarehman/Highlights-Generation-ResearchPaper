 The aim of this study was to assess the pharmacokinetic profile of LFX in sheep after intravenous and oral administration of 2mg kg LFX once a day for 5days and to evaluate its tissue depletion in the muscles heart liver lungs and kidneys . Twenty healthy female sheep were randomly divided into two equal groups . Each group was further randomly subdivided into two equal subgroups

@highlight Pharmacokinetic estimates after intravenous and oral administration were comparable
@highlight The highest concentration of the drug was in the kidney and liver
@highlight Levofloxacin did not accumulate in plasma or organs
