 Foot pain is a frequent cause of lameness in horses and can involve multiple structures within the hoof . The T ligament is poorly described .

@highlight The T ligament transverse lamina proximal sesamoid ligament connects the deep digital flexor tendon the middle phalanx.
@highlight It connects indirectly the proximal border of the navicular bone via the junction of both collateral sesamoidean ligaments.
@highlight It consists of a core of densely packed elastic fibres surrounded by loose connective tissue blood vessels.
@highlight It is covered by surrounding synovia of the equine digital extremity.
@highlight This ligament is a dorsal vinculum for the deep digital flexor tendon.
