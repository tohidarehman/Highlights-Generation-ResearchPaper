 Ventricular arrhythmias are often incidental or coincidental with systemic disease . Ventricular arrhythmias are also the most likely cause of many sudden cardiac deaths in horses . This dichotomy creates challenges in the management of horses with VAs . This review presents current knowledge of diagnosis prognosis and treatment of VAs in horses .
