 Clearance of measles virus is complex . Infectious virus is cleared by the adaptive immune response manifested by the characteristic maculopapular rash . CD8

@highlight In immunocompromised patients failure of measles virus clearance may lead to progressive lung or nervous system disease.
@highlight Children infected at a young age may develop subacute sclerosing panencephalitis a progressive nervous system disease many years later.
@highlight Slow clearance of measles virus RNA from lymphoid tissue is associated with maturation of immune responses and life long immunity.
@highlight Slow clearance of measles virus RNA from lymphoid tissue may impair new immune responses and increase susceptibility to other infections.
