@highlight Mixing of a viscoplastic fluid using Scaba 6SRGT impellers in a cylindrical vessel is studied.
@highlight Cuts are introduced in each blade of the impeller in order to reduce power requirements.
@highlight The effects of the cut height cut length and of the number of cuts are explored.
@highlight A reduction in power number until 66 was obtained by using the cut technique.
