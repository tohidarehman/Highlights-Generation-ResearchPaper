 Multicomponent transition metal compounds with multiple anions have been actively researched due to their superior electrochemical properties . They transform into heterostructured materials with different bandgaps during electrochemical reactions . This study is the first to introduce metal hydroxysulfide as an efficient anode material for use in lithium ion batteries . A model compound cobalt hydroxysulfide was employed to explore certain conversion reaction mechanisms with Li ions .
