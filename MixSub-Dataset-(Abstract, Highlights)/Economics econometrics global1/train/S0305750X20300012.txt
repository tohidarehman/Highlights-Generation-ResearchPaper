 This paper highlights new findings on the wage productivity nexus in the World Factory Economy . After presenting the long run macro elasticity characterizing the phase of Chinese economic development since the eighties we look at the wage productivity nexus from a micro level perspective using a detailed firm level dataset covering the period of ownership restructuring . A few results are quite robust under different estimation strategies .
