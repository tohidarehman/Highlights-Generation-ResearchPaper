 Defining and measuring competitiveness remains a subject of interest as well as debate policy makers need to understand how competitive their country is relative to others and how their competitive position evolves overtime

@highlight This paper proposes a multidimensional framework of firm competitiveness.
@highlight Factor analysis validates the competitiveness framework using firm level data on over 100 countries.
@highlight The index of competitiveness positively correlates with proxies of firm competitiveness.
@highlight The competitiveness framework is applicable to firms of different size and export status.
