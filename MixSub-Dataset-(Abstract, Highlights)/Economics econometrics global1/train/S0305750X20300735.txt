 Climate related asset stranding refers to the depreciation of assets such as resource reserves infrastructure or industries resulting from the unanticipated changes such as the tightening of climate policies . Although developing countries especially fossil fuel exporters may be most concerned by this issue its analysis in development has so far been limited .
